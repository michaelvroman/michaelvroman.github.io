Volunteer Service and Affiliation

It has been one of my hobbies to volunteer for any type of community service in San Diego. Some of the organizations that I have volunteered at are listed below.

San Diego Food Bank. Active volunteer member since 2019.

As a volunteer member, you will be given a variety of task such as cleaning and packaging fresh produce for immediate distribution and assemble food packages for vulnerable families and seniors. I usually volunteer on weekends. They log the number of hours you volunteer for tracking purposes.

Learn more

Hands-on San Diego. Active volunteer member since 2019.

This organization offers volunteer projects that you can join or even lead a project. Such projects include beach and park clean-up, serve meals to the homeless, and distribute or sort food and supplies. To sign up, you will have to pay a one-time $25 fee. What I love about this is you get to meet people from all walks of life and learn from them.

Learn more

Arc of San Diego. Active volunteer member since 2019.

Its commitment is to support and empower children and adults with disabilities to achieve their goals and reach their maximum potential. They seldom have volunteer opportunities on weekends which may depend on the number of staffs available for support.
